Tip 1: Order Does Not Matter提示 1: 順序不重要

A X B = B X A

Example: 示例：1 X 2 = 2, and 2 X 1 = 2

So, don't memorize both "1 X 2" and "1 X 2", just memorize that "a 1 and a 2 make 2" when multiplied.

Tip 2: Memorization提示2：記憶

Train your memory!訓練你的記憶！

When you can simply remember the multiplication tables,your life will be a lot easier.當您記得乘法表時，您的生活就會輕鬆很多。

It is too hard to memorize the whole table at once. So you can learn it in "chunks".這實在是太難能一次記住整個表。所以，你可以學習它在“塊”。
